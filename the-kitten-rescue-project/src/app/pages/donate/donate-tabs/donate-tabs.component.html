Many Employers participate in a charitable donation matching program.

If your Employer does participate in a program like this, they may match up to 100% of your charitable donation to an official non-profit like us.
This can be very helpful to us, as we are able to help more kittens with the help of you and your employer.

To learn more about charitable matching programs please reach out to your Employer's HR team, or read more about it in the link below.
